<p>I think there's a worthwhile compromise where you implement <code>ISet&lt;T&gt;</code> and <code>IReadOnlyList&lt;T&gt;</code>. This exposes the ordering efficiently (a caller can get the nth item in constant time rather than time linear in n) without committing you to the mutating methods of <code>List&lt;T&gt;</code>.</p>
