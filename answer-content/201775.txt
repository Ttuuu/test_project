<p>I'll just add that <code>CharToHex</code> assumes that the high bytes of <code>EAX</code> and <code>EBX</code> are zero.  In particular, the <code>AH</code> value will be non-zero for an input string length greater than 255 characters.  When you look up the digit with <code>[Digits+eax]</code> you can access out of bounds memory.</p>
