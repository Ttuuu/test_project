<h2>Naming</h2>
<p>Yes, I know that the assignment gives single letter variables, but that's the way of the mathematician, not the way of the programmer. A programmer needs to be able to see in an instant what a variable or method is referring to.</p>
<p>In this case, I would use the variable names <code>firstPosition</code>, <code>secondPosition</code>, <code>firstSpeed</code> and <code>secondSpeed</code>.</p>
