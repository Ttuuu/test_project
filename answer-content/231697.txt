<p>With numeric tasks that could yield a wide range of answers, more informative to use <code>"%e"</code> than <code>"%f"</code> - else small non-zero values appear as <code>"0.000000"</code>.</p>

<pre><code>// printf("min y is %f",min_y);
printf("min y is %e\n",min_y);
</code></pre>
