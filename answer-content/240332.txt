<p><strong><code>**</code></strong> this means power , for example : <code>5 ** 2</code> which mean : <code>5 squared --&gt; 25</code>. <br><code>2 ** 7</code> which mean : <code>2 to the power of 7 -- &gt; 128</code>.</p>

<p><strong><code>//</code></strong> this means Floor division - division that results into whole number adjusted to the left in the number line.
<br>For example:</p>

<pre><code>x = 15
y = 4
print('x / y =',x/y)   # Output: x / y = 3.75
print('x // y =',x//y) # Output: x // y = 3
print('x ** y =',x**y) # Output: x ** y = 50625
print('x * y =',x*y)   # Output: x * y = 60
</code></pre>

<p><strong>Update</strong>
<br>To be more clear :  <code>*</code> this means multiplication operator.</p>
