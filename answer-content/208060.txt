<p>To check if date is today, use Android utils library</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>DateUtils.isToday(long timeInMilliseconds)</p>
</blockquote>

<p>This utils class also offers human readable strings for relative times. For example,</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>DateUtils.getRelativeTimeSpanString(long timeInMilliseconds) -&gt; "42
  minutes ago"</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The are several parameters you can play with to define how precise the time span should be</p>
