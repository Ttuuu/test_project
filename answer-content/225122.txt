<p>You use of <code>std::vector&lt;std::string&gt;</code> for the arrays of string constants is wasteful. Both <code>std::vector</code> and <code>std::string</code> are dynamic types that can potentially allocate. A much more lightweight choice would be <code>constexpr std::array&lt;const char*&gt;</code> or <code>constexpr std::array&lt;std::string_view&gt;</code>.</p>
