<p>One improvement over @tobias_k s answer is to add the extra upper bound on b of <code>b&lt;1 + a*a//2</code>. This is valid because after that, the distance between <code>b^2</code> and <code>(b+1)^2</code> will be bigger than <code>a^2</code>, so <code>c^2</code> does not exist. This provides a massive speedup at least a first.</p>
