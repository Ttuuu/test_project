<p>You should save all files that are downloaded by <code>wget</code> in a local directory, either called <code>distfiles</code>, or <code>download</code>, or even <code>cache</code>. This makes upgrading independent of the internet being available, and also prevents unnecessary duplicate downloads. Plus, it allows the downloaded files to be checked whether they are <a href="https://www.mediawiki.org/keys/keys.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">cryptographically signed</a>.</p>
