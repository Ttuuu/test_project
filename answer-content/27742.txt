<p>If code is syntactically the same, it is the same code...  I think you are over-thinking this.  The two code examples above are different enough to warrant their existence.</p>

<p>Now, if you wanted to eliminate two methods that did the exact same thing, but lived in two places, that is simple.  Just delete the one with less usage, then replace all calls to the old method with the new one.  This is incredibly simple if they share the same method signature, slightly more involved if they do not.</p>
