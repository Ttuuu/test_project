<p>You can simplify <code>foo = foo + ...</code> to just <code>foo += ...</code>.</p>
<p>For example you can simplify the following two lines:</p>
<blockquote>
<pre><code>m = m + 1
</code></pre>
</blockquote>
<pre><code>m += 1
</code></pre>
<blockquote>
<pre><code>total = total + tenttotal + ref[roman[m]] - negtotal
</code></pre>
</blockquote>
<pre><code>total += tenttotal + ref[roman[m]] - negtotal
</code></pre>
    </div>
