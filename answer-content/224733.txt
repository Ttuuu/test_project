<p>To make your code short, understandable, elegant, maintainable, and fast, I recommend to:</p>

<ul>
<li>implement separately syntax and semantics</li>
<li>for syntax part, construct recursive descent parser. </li>
<li>read something on theory, starting from <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backus%E2%80%93Naur_form" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Bacus-Naur form</a></li>
<li>learn examples on <a href="https://github.com/search?l=Java&amp;q=recursive%20descent%20parser&amp;type=Repositories" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Github recursive descent parser</a></li>
<li>ask questions at Stackoverflow. Tag them with [recursive-descent].</li>
</ul>
