<p>You could have a collection of ValueConverters which would validate your TextInputs' strings. For example one could be a PhoneNumberValidator or PhoneToBoolConverter (example names there). This way HasError would bind to <code>HasError="{Binding PhoneNumber, Converter={StaticResource PhoneNumberValidator}}"</code>. Converters are handy and you can reuse them in multiple places around your code if they are declared in your <code>App.xaml.cs</code> for example. </p>
