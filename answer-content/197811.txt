<p>Why not save yourself all those ADO.NET and Datatable to custom class headaches and use an ORM like <a href="https://github.com/StackExchange/Dapper" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Dapper</a>? And then you simply have a bunch of Services, e.g. an <code>AlbumService</code> which has a <code>Get(int id)</code> method, etc. And you can then have an <code>IAlbumService</code> so you can do IoC/DI etc.</p>
