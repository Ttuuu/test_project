<h3>More bugs</h3>
<p>In addition to the bugs already mentioned by others, here are two more:</p>
<ol>
<li><p>If an array consists of just one positive and one negative number, such as <code>{ 5, -2 }</code>, your function will return zero instead of <code>-10</code>.</p>
</li>
<li><p>If the array contains large integers, your product will overflow when you multiply.  You should use <code>long</code> variables instead of <code>int</code> to do your multiplications, and your function should return a <code>long</code>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
