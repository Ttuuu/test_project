<p>The line <code>v = kwargs.get(k) or v</code> means you cannot use the <code>update</code> method to set any of the properties to 0. <code>get</code> can take an additional argument for the default value to return. If you change that line to <code>v = kwargs.get(k, v)</code>, then <code>price.update(sale=0)</code> works as expected.</p>
