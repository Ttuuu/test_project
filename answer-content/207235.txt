<p>An easy win would be to <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office/vba/api/excel.application.screenupdating" rel="nofollow noreferrer">disable screen updating</a>. This will cause your script to run faster, as excel will not try and rerender as your macro runs. I've found this can speed up tasks that involve spreadsheet data insertion significantly. Be sure to re-enable screen updating if your script hits an error, otherwise it can be troublesome to turn on again.</p>
