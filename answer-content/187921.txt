<p>Think of the phrase <code>: public</code> to mean "is a".</p>

<p>A dog is not a kennel, nor is a cat, so <code>Dog</code> and <code>Cat</code> should not inherit from <code>Kennel</code>. You need a class called <code>Animal</code>. Cats and dogs are animals, so <code>Cat</code> and <code>Dog</code> should derive from <code>Animal</code>. Animals are contained in a kennel.</p>
