<p>In captchas, there are times where letters are overlapping, in this case it would be considered to be only one letter because of the algorithms you use. You should consider <a href="https://docs.opencv.org/2.4/doc/tutorials/imgproc/erosion_dilatation/erosion_dilatation.html" rel="nofollow noreferrer">eroding</a> your image to try to detach letters when they are slightly overlapping. If they overlap a lot, you'll need to find another algorithm.</p>
