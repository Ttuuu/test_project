<p>I wouldn't go the <code>StringBuilder</code> route, simply because you need to handle the "what if I'm at the start/end" problem. Instead, simply collect the "URL_translated" key-value pairs into an <code>IEnumerable&lt;string&gt;</code> and then apply <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/api/system.string.join" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><code>string.join()</code></a>.</p>

<p>If you're working in .NET Core, <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/api/Microsoft.AspNetCore.WebUtilities.QueryHelpers" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Microsoft does all the lifting</a> for you. Here's <a href="https://github.com/aspnet/AspNetCore/blob/master/src/Http/WebUtilities/src/QueryHelpers.cs" rel="nofollow noreferrer">how they do it</a>.</p>
