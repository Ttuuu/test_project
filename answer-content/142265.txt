<h1>Style</h1>

<p>Make your code more readable so that you can more easily reason about its logic.</p>

<ol>
<li>Use names that have meaning (e.g. <code>minimum_length</code> instead of <code>m</code>)</li>
<li>Don't create needless aliases (e.g. <code>for length in [minimum_length, maximum_length]</code> instead of <code>for l in leng</code>)</li>
</ol>
