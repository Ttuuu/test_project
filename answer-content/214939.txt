<p>Yes, using ETS for this sort of temporary data is totally fine, as long as you realize of course that all that intermediate state is lost whenever you restart your server. In fact, if you dig into Phoenix and Elixir a bit, you will see a lot of ETS usage - it's not so much a thing you turn to in exceptional circumstances, but more a tool in the extensive toolbox at the same level of, say, <code>GenServer</code>. </p>
