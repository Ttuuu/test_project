<p>Just right off the bat, you describe lists about your cards as sets. </p>

<p>Lists are different than sets. That's why your teacher is saying you didn't use sets. Sets are created with <code>{}</code> curly brackets, not <code>[]</code> square brackets. What's what is, of course, asked &amp; answered on <a href="https://stackoverflow.com/a/4407915/1188513">Stack Overflow</a>.</p>
