<p>A 60 GB file fits a <strong>lot</strong> of domains.</p>

<p>Reporting progress after each of them results in so many calls, that the application may end up spending more time reporting the progress than doing useful work. </p>

<p>Consider reporting the progress less often, say only in 1 or 0.1% increments.</p>
