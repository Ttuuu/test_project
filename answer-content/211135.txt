<h1><em><strong>FUNCTIONS!</strong></em></h1>
<p>Divide your code into functions to make it more readable. In a couple of places, your code is <em>12 levels of indentation</em> deep! That's the equivalent of 48 characters!</p>
<hr>
<h1>Spacing</h1>
<p>Operators should usually have a space on either side.</p>
<h1><code>with</code></h1>
<p>The best way of opening files is as follows (to make sure it get closed even if there is an error):</p>
<pre><code>with open(...) as f:
    ...
</code></pre>
