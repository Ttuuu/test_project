<p>You should add <code>cryptoStream.Close()</code> inside your <code>CryptoStream()</code> when you are finished with it. Otherwise you may end up with mismatching <code>byte[] size</code> when decrypting and you may get an exception on <code>cryptoStream.Read(encrypted, 0 , length)</code> and/or a corrupted result.</p>
