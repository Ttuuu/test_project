<p>This might be a peripheral advice  </p>

<p>using <code>std::cout</code> is a <strong>slow procedure</strong>, you may do better with defining a temporary buffer of some significant size and use <code>std::cout</code> as soon as the buffer fills then reuse the buffer.</p>
