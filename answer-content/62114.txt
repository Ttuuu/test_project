<p>It's not valid HTML5 to have a <code>&lt;header&gt;</code> element inside a <code>&lt;footer&gt;</code> element. You could have checked this yourself on this site:</p>

<p><a href="http://validator.w3.org/check" rel="nofollow noreferrer">http://validator.w3.org/check</a></p>

<p>For using <code>&lt;header&gt;</code>, <code>&lt;footer&gt;</code> and <code>&lt;section&gt;</code> correctly, see this page:</p>

<p><a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/footer" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/footer</a></p>

<p>For even more technical details, see the HTML5 specification:</p>

<p><a href="http://www.w3.org/TR/html5/sections.html#the-footer-element" rel="nofollow noreferrer">http://www.w3.org/TR/html5/sections.html#the-footer-element</a></p>
