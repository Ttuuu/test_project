<p>Just a word on password strength: the string '$]1V9q' should not get rated 70% safe. It shouldn't even be rated any percentage safe, because it's a very weak password. Length is the decisive factor in password strength and should weigh far more strongly than other element. Even though the website is originally at fault here for using an inaccurate algorithm, you should avoid making the same mistake!</p>
