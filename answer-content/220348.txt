<p>Frankly, if your repositories don't have state (i.e. <code>private</code> member variables), their methods should be marked as <code>static</code>, and ergo, each repository class should be marked as <code>static</code> and you have no need to worry about instantiation anywhere. They're essentially utility functions to be called whenever you need them.</p>
