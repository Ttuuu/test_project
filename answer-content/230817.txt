<p>Perfect naming in any language allowing freedom for your names are:</p>

<ol>
<li>Descriptive. (Makes it clear what it means at a glance)</li>
<li>Short. (less chance for typos, types faster and therefore easier to work with.)</li>
</ol>

<p>In your case, the names only mean to affect the text size. So appropriate names would be something like:</p>

<pre class="lang-css prettyprint-override"><code>text-medium {
    font-size: 1.4rem;
}
text-big {
    font-size: 1.6rem;
}
</code></pre>

<p>Since this makes it clear what they do. </p>
