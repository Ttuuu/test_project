<p>This is a very common interview question. However, your algorithm won't suffice: the array may be unsorted.</p>

<p>The method is to find the sum of the numbers in the array and then subtract it from the sum of numbers from 1 through 100. What's left over is what is missing from a complete list 1..100.</p>

<p>Sum of natural numbers <span class="math-container">\$1..N\ = \dfrac{N(N+1)}{2}\$</span>.</p>
